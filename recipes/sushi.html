How to Make Salmon Rolls

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe on the website- but here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make salmon sushi at home:

  1. Soak and cook the rice, then mix the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate.
  2. Assemble the sushi rolls based on the detailed recipe on the website
  3. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces
